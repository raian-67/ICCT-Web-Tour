Welcome to ICCT Colleges

A Global Pinoy Distinction

From its inception in 1992, ICCT Colleges has grown from a single campus into eight campuses across the Province of Rizal, with upcoming expansions in Rodriguez and Tanay. It offers Bachelor Programs with CHED recognition, TechVoc programs by TESDA, Certificate Programs, E-Learning Programs, and Senior High School (Grade 11 & 12) under DepEd.

Our dedicated faculty and staff, modern digital services, and updated facilities ensure that students gain the knowledge and skills needed for the workplace of tomorrow. ICCT promotes holistic development through academic excellence, community outreach programs, tree planting, waste segregation, sports events, and medical-dental missions.

We invite you to join our community of learning and begin a rewarding and exciting journey with us.
